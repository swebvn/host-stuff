Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

What services does EMBRACECANVAS offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of web services including custom website design, web application development, e-commerce solutions, and reliable web hosting services.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and requirements. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we typically need to know your project goals, desired features, timeline, and budget range. We'll guide you through our discovery process to collect all necessary information.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, and technical support.

What hosting options do you provide?

We offer various hosting packages tailored to different needs, from shared hosting for small websites to dedicated servers for high-traffic applications. All our hosting services include robust security measures and 24/7 monitoring.
