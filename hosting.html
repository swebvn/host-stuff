Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our hosting services.

Can I migrate my existing website to your hosting?

Yes, we offer free website migration services with all our hosting plans. Our team will handle the entire migration process to ensure a smooth transition with minimal downtime.

What control panel do you offer?

We provide industry-standard control panels (cPanel, Plesk, or DirectAdmin) depending on your hosting plan, making it easy to manage your website, domains, email accounts, and databases.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes, all our hosting plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

How often do you back up my website?

The backup frequency depends on your hosting plan. Our shared hosting plans include daily backups, while our Business and Enterprise plans include more frequent backups. We keep multiple backup copies for added security.

What happens if I exceed my resource limits?

We monitor resource usage and will notify you if you're approaching your limits. If you consistently exceed your current plan's resources, we'll recommend upgrading to a more suitable plan. We never abruptly shut down your website due to occasional traffic spikes.
